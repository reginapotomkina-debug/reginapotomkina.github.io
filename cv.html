Statement

I am a visual artist and graphic designer based in Frankfurt am Main, currently studying Fine Art with a focus on painting at Kunsthochschule Kassel in the class of Prof. Dierk Schmidt. My work engages with Jewish lived realities in Germany, my own biography as a Jewish contingent refugee from Ukraine, and the people and objects of my immediate surroundings.

Painting serves as a means to connect personal experience with collective memory and cultural symbols — negotiating questions of identity, belonging, and continuity.
