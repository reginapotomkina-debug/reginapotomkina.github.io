Visual Artist & Painter

Regina
Potomkina

My work engages with Jewish lived realities in Germany, my own biography as a Jewish contingent refugee from Ukraine, and the people and objects of my immediate surroundings. Painting serves as a means to connect personal experience with collective memory and cultural symbols — negotiating questions of identity, belonging, and continuity.

Studies at Kunsthochschule Kassel, class of Prof. Dierk Schmidt. Based in Frankfurt am Main.
